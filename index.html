This 3D thematic map shows the number of noise complaints in New York City according to all the 3-1-1 Service requests from January 1st to March 1st, 2017. The data was directly downloaded from NYC OpenData. In the United States, 3-1-1 is a special telephone number that provides access to non-emergency municipal services.
